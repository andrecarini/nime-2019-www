New Interfaces for Musical Expression

Universidade Federal do Rio Grande do Sul
Porto Alegre, Brazil
7-10 June, 2019

New Interfaces for Musical Expression, also known as NIME, is an international conference dedicated to scientific research on the development of new technologies and their role in musical expression and artistic performance. Researchers and musicians from all over the world gather to share their knowledge and late-breaking work on new musical interface design.

The conference began as a workshop in 2001 and, since then, has been held annually around the world. The 19th edition of NIME will be hosted at the Universidade Federal do Rio Grande do Sul in Porto Alegre, Brazil, and will hold lectures, installations, concerts, and workshops.

Review phase

Please note the submissions are now closed and we are now in the review phase. We anticipate sending out notifications by mid-March. A reminder that workshop and demo submissions deadline is March 1st.

Review Process Update

We are pleased to report this year we have had a total of 367 submissions, including 164 papers, 137 music, 35 workshops and demos, and 31 installations. We expect sending out the notifications over the next two weeks.
