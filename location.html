WELCOME TO PORTO ALEGRE!

The conference will take place at the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul (UFRGS), from June 3rd to June 6th, 2019. It will be almost completely located in Porto Alegre.

UFRGS, one of the largest federal universities in Brazil and among the highest rated, has one of the highest numbers of scientific publications.

The academic community of UFRGS brings together over 27,000 undergraduate students, over 9,300 graduate students and more than 2,500 professors and lecturers.
