WELCOME TO PORTO ALEGRE!

The conference will take place at the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul from Jun 3rd to Jun 6th, 2019.

The Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul - UFRGS is one of the largest federal universities in Brazil. It is almost completely located in Porto Alegre. UFRGS is among the most highly rated Brazilian universities, and has one of the highest numbers of scientific publications. UFRGS has over 27,000 undergraduate students, over 9,300 graduate students, and more than 2,500 professors/lecturers.
